      About

      Ellis. Bay View kid. Now eighteen.

      I just turned eighteen. I grew up two blocks from where you probably live. My brother and I are both Wyatts, and Wyatt Auto Detailing is ours.

      I got into detailing when I got my first car. It looked rough. I got to work on it and made it look brand new. That moment is what started all of this. I realized every car has that potential, and I wanted to show other people what their vehicle is actually capable of.

      The work itself is two-bucket wash with grit guards, microfiber sorted by job, hand wax, or a ceramic coat on Premium. Interior gets vacuumed, wiped down, glass, and door jambs. On the lighter packages, I won't machine-polish a panel without asking you first.
